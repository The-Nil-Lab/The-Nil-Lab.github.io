Our Approach

We immerse ourselves in the community’s environment while designing and delivering solutions to them.

We use the non-linear approach of design thinking to understand the individual and community contexts and design sustainable solutions. Our understanding of people’s behaviour triggers us to devise solutions that are context specific, developed after careful diagnostics and following an iterative process of design, testing, and adoption.
